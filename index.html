About My Journey

Assalamu Alaikum. I am Nur Mohammod Manik, a professional Software Engineer and seasoned Data Entry Specialist based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. My career is built on a foundation of rigorous academic learning and hands-on technical expertise. I completed my early education at Haji Abdur Rahim International School up to the 8th grade, which sparked my initial interest in the digital world. Following this, I pursued professional excellence by completing a comprehensive one-year Data Entry Professional Certification from the Master IT Institute.

To transition into high-level engineering, I underwent three years of intensive virtual training in Software Engineering and Coding from Lranvran IT India. This experience equipped me with the skills to architect complex systems, manage enterprise-level servers, and develop scalable web applications. Today, I am proud to be a Software Engineering Class Teacher, where I share my knowledge and mentor aspiring developers in mastering modern coding practices.

My expertise isn't just limited to creating new systems; I am also a specialist in Website Redesign and Modification. I have the ability to take an existing platform and transform its UI/UX, optimize its performance, and add advanced functionalities to meet international standards. Currently, I lead two major technology ventures: CNI Host, which provides robust hosting infrastructure, and RYPAY Digital, an automated payment solution. With a 200 TB storage infrastructure and thousands of active users, my goal is to provide world-class tech services with complete integrity and transparency.
